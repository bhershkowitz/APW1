APW Project

My project idea this year is superhero themed! I will be making a variety of things from a wide range of heroes from Spider-Man to Batman to Ironman. I am starting off with Spider-Man and his webshooters.

The Idea

The concept of this design is somewhat simple compared to what others have done online. The webshooter will be a small railgun. A railgun uses electromagnetic force to launch an object. What the plan is for this project is to launch a small object attached to a string to mimic the web.

The parts

-copper rod
-copper wire
-transformers
-capacitors
-diodes
-momentary switches
